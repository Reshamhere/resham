EP — 01
The Developer

Hi. I'm Resham.

I'm a developer who believes every line of code is a micro-decision about what the world should feel like. I build apps, tools, and occasionally chaotic experiments — all in the name of learning.

My stack changes. My curiosity doesn't. Whether it's Swift on iOS or JavaScript on the web, I care about the end experience — the feeling someone gets when something just works beautifully.

I'm in my builder era. Shipping things. Breaking things. Unlearning the wrong lessons and relearning better ones.

🔭
Curious by default
If I don't understand how something works, I build a version of it until I do.
🧩
Creative problem solver
I don't look for the obvious answer. I look for the one that makes someone smile.
⚡
Slightly chaotic
Three side projects, one main focus, zero regrets. Chaos is just creativity without labels.
🌱
Perpetual learner
Currently learning what I don't know. Which is, honestly, still a lot.
